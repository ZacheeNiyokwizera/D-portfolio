About me

He started writing songs when he was still in primary school grade 3 with his first hip hop song entitled ( Ndumwana Mwiza ), unfortunately he never got a chance to record it.He has humanitarian experience, he volunteered for Save The Children International and worked for Alight formerly the American Refugee Committee (ARC) and he as well won 2018 ChangeMaker Award as a finalist. He is taking Internet application and web development at the college. His various talented works can be found in his Instagram Bio. He does enjoy exploring new places and meeting new people. The most important thing, he is a simple man with simple life and dreams. His every day prayer, is “may God show him how to never lose hope in the midst of life challenges.”
